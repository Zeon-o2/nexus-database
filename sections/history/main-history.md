# Chapter I
Dark, just dark. Everything I saw, heard, felt, was the nothingness of the dark. That was the only sentence I thought of when I opened my eyes. I woke up alert, but also confused because I didn't remember what could have happened before. I soon realized, they drugged me with some kind of strong tranquilizer. I spent a few minutes wandering around and recognizing what environment I was in. Through a CCTV camera, I saw the purple triangle, 'NEXUS'.
-Ah, shit...
Then, metallic footsteps were approaching the small cell in which I was. Anxiety took over me, "they're coming to kill me, Mr. Nexus has had enough of this", the heavy boots, the smell of metallic and inhuman powder, everything in that cell was one thing. Control. Then I saw the upright figure of 2 guards, with no part of the body showing. 'Their uniforms seem to have changed', I thought in the midst of the chaos. A harsh, unison voice broke through the deafening hum of silence.
-Follow us.
-Hmph... 
I got up and followed them, even with the immense desire not to obey. I was pulled and then dragged through the corridors, by arms that I had the impression of being robotic. After a few hallways, they opened the last containment door, which led to a completely metallic hallway, with the solid appearance that would survive a nuclear winter and pale white neon lights. It didn't feel like a place for humans. And there they stopped. The guard to my right made a short hand signal, then moved forward slowly. I had my head down all the way, but I still managed to memorize the way. Ahead was a solid door divided by 2 diagonal pieces. It was well polished, reflecting the cold light from the ceiling.
We walked with hard steps. The door opened as I got closer, and I caught a glimpse of a memory as I saw the room. It was a room full of transparent screens, from the lower corners to the entire ceiling. There was a chair right in the center, slightly tilted upwards, which forced me to sit down. I stood in my chair, analyzing everything. My thoughts were clouded, but my memory had returned just in time. They had neutralized me before I could regain ▮▮▮▮▮. The two guards waited at the door, and then all the screens were lighting up at the same time, to find a holographic projection of a bluish sphere that reflected throughout the room. I remembered immediately. _N.O.V.A_.
