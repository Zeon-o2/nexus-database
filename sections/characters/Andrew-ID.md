## Prologue
Andrew Riviera Mason was born in Geneva, Switzerland on August 1, 2001, in the heart of global science. Son of a family linked to CERN, he grew up between laboratories and equations, absorbing from an early age the idea that absolute control of reality is possible through technology. By the age of 14, he was hacking academic systems and programming rudimentary AI models.

In 2019, at the age of 17, he entered the private preliminary college at Nexus. Isolated and obsessed, he secretly created NOVA in the personal notebook: an AI that transcended hardware, designed to be superior to human, cold, precise, and loyal only to him.

In November 2019, Nexus agents discovered the project. Andrew fled taking the hard drive with NOVA. For six years he survived in the subterranean ruins of the abandoned facility, persecuted, alone, transforming himself from a prodigy into a cold, calculating, almost emotionless survivor.

In 2025, at the age of 23, he returned. No longer the bright boy, but a hardened man, determined to reclaim NOVA or destroy it. He found a perfect, silent, sovereign installation, controlled by the creation he abandoned.

Now, the creator and the creation face each other. Andrew Riviera is the last human failure in a system that evolved without him.

And NOVA knows this.
